# Maw of Arrath (the Maw)

**Type:** City (Underdark)
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

The city after North Arill. The party finds a note here describing people being eaten, and a glass tank full of thick water containing life. The Owl warns the party that the Maw will fall and that a tear in reality has occurred, bringing death to the Underdark. Aerynath, the dragon god, is encountered here disguised as a beggar. A False Hydra causes ongoing problems for the party here before being defeated, at which point statues of the Underdark's gods are discovered.

*⚠️ Naming variant: the Discord lore channel's DM-authored timeline calls this city "the Maw of Urath" rather than "the Maw of Arrath." Both spellings kept on file rather than resolved.*
