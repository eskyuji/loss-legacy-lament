# Norathin

**Type:** City (Underdark)
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

Home to Behelor (and possibly Lehinar), a Moa who seeks an audience with Paavu as the newest Moa. Ruby the Glass Eye is found staying here at the Four Flumphs, where Iphitus spots her and flees. One of several locations Ruby names as a possible Moa location.

---

## Notable Locations & Businesses

- **Gelatinous Cube** — a vacation-home-style inn.
- **The Four Flumphs** — the better inn, a hot-springs resort with actual flumphs in residence; everyone feels calm while there.
