**Type:** City (Underdark)
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

## Overview

A small city — the first stop in the Underdark after Gol'talum, followed by North and South Arill.

---

## Notable Locations & Businesses

- **The Coat Hanger** — inn, usually not busy. Barry the Bard tends bar here.
- **Boar's Hat** — the other inn in town.
- **Shattered Vial** — the local alchemist's shop.
