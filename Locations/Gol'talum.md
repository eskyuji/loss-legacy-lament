# Gol'talum

**Type:** City
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

The last checkpoint/city before entering the Underdark. A wild-west frontier town where two major families fight for control, with a mix of surface and Underdark citizens. Home to a fight club where Paavu nearly dies fighting a dragon turtle, earning exactly one fan (the halfling Pharaoh). Jorn Hall the Paladin, a Lizardfolk priest of Urath, can be found here.

---

## Notable Locations & Businesses

- **The Poisoned Dagger** — tavern where the original Silkworm tells the party to seek him out; also where a man in a gold-trimmed cloak sat with Silkworm when he gave the party their original mission.
- **Fight club** — underground arena where Paavu fights a dragon turtle.
