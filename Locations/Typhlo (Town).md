# Typhlo (Town)

**Type:** Town / sacred site (Underdark)
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A town built near the volcano where the god Typhlo buried himself after his battle with Urath — the battle that created the Underdark itself. The Discord lore channel identifies Typhlo and Urath as Twin Creator Gods of the Underdark, Avatars of Fire and Water respectively (Typhlo fire, Urath water) — a fuller framing than the player notes' "Urath was killed by Typhlo," which this doesn't necessarily contradict but does recontextualize as a battle between equals rather than a one-sided kill. A large crack has appeared in the tomb of Typhlo. A water genasi living in the town is revealed by Yannick's truesight to be a "Blood of the Beyond."

---

## Notable Locations & Businesses

- **Typhlo's Crown** — an inn built where Typhlo's head should be.
- **The Trail of Sooth** — supposedly the path Typhlo dragged his sword along on the way to his final battle with Urath.
