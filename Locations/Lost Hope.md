# Lost Hope

**Type:** City  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn, CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World

---

## Overview

A city led by Tin, "the Merchant King." Per The Magus's Oathstone research, Lost Hope may share an Oathstone with the city of Rath, the way The Crash shares one with the Azure. Tin's forces arrive in Shaidar's Rest in the aftermath of the blood magic riot to formally pledge solidarity, and Tin hints that Lost Hope may need the Frost Shepherds' help in turn before long.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

The Frost Shepherds' Session 0.5 journey takes them through Lost Hope itself, including its business district and an altar on Tyn Street — named for Tyn, the Oathstone of Tenacity, who has been present since the city was little more than a fifty-foot patch of dirt. Tyn gives his sword Reno to Bas Tetran XXIV here before the city goes dark around him, echoing the same darkening fate that befalls Shaidar's Rest elsewhere in the campaign.
