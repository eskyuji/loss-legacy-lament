# The Maw

**Type:** Region / dark territory
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

A region that has gone dark, home to a False Hydra and the site of Desiree Redd's rescue. Desiree was found imprisoned in a temple here, used as a vessel by a version of Feit, and recovered by December's search party.
