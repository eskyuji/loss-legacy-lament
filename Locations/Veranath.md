# Veranath

**Type:** City (ruin)
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

A dead, abandoned city now ruled by a cat hive-mind under Thelonius, the Oathstone of Remembrance. Home to the Gates of the Afterlife, which the party crosses into the death-world ("Mythrir -1") to meet Enki Steelbane, Un'Malo, and the All-Seeing. Thelonius leads the party through four labors here as part of his Oathstone of Remembrance arc.

*⚠️ Possible naming overlap: this city's spelling, "Veranath," closely echoes [Locations/Varaneth.md](../archive/Locations/Varaneth) — the Campaign 2 capital of the Eternal Empire, also strongly tied to Thelonius (he holds court there at the Cat's Pajamas). Given the roughly 2,000-year gap between Campaign 2 and Campaign 4, this may be the same city fallen to ruin, or a separate place that merely sounds alike. Filed as a separate entry rather than merged; flagging the overlap on both files rather than resolving it.*
