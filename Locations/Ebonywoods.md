# Ebonywoods

**Type:** Forest / region  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

Birthplace of Enki Steelbane, a six-foot Tortle druid. The site of his parents' murder at the hands of the dragonborn Hagorn the Red — the event that drove Enki to join the Empire's military (The Fist) and seek the strength to one day face his killer.
