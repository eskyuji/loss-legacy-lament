# Hall of the Owls

**Type:** Ancient ruin / dungeon  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

An ancient tower-dungeon holding the remains and lingering spirits of the original Moa — Un'Malo, Ryx, Paavu, Iphitus, and Behelor among them — preserved as both statues and (in some cases) simultaneous living/dead forms. Guarded by tentacle creatures from the Beyond. Tyra, the Second Sword of Typhlo (or possibly Urath — see her file for the naming discrepancy), has spent an eternity here fighting them as penance for the deaths of her friends.

The Ring of Ryx, taken from Ryx's remains, functions as a key to the Hall.

---

## Notable Loot

- Chitin Swordbreaker, Chitin armor, and a shield looted from the animated statue of Un'Malo by Taeus, Abacus, and Enki respectively.
- An urn dedicated to Nagi, demigod of fungus and rot, near which Taeus plants Myconid spores as a gesture of respect.
