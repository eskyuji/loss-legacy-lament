# Gul'Malar

**Type:** City (Underdark)
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A city reached by hiring the minotaur guides Mogus, Firesong, and Sunspeaker to cross the desert from Gol'talum. Home to Xanathar, a Moa who runs the Xanathar thieves' guild, and to Addie's Acquisitions, where Paavu is sent for information. One of several locations Ruby the Glass Eye names as a possible Moa location.

---

## Notable Locations & Businesses

- **The Empty Mug** — not a very nice inn.
- **Addie's Acquisitions** — run by Addie; Paavu is sent here by the potion seller Severus to learn about the Xanathar guild. Barry the Parrot is kicked out when Paavu requests a private audience with Addie.
