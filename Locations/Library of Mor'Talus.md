# Library of Mor'Talus

**Type:** Domain / library  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

*The Campaign 1 raw notes ("Guilds in the Dark") reference a deathly figure called "Mor'Talus" in the Underdark — human-looking, flanked by demons — consistent with this same god under his fuller, later-established identity.*

---

## Overview

The domain of Mortalus (Mort), death god and son of Un'Malo, who serves as its keeper and scholar. A repository of knowledge fitting Mort's composed, intellectual presentation — though his illusion drops to reveal a feathered-winged skeleton beneath at moments of great grief, such as his father's death.
