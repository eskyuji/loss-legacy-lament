# Goroshi

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

An ancient Oni — one of ten, each tied to a region of the world. Appears as a massive figure with skin of liquid copper and flat black eyes. The Brown Oni, for whom the inn in The Crash is named. The land itself is an extension of him. Cannot kill, cannot lie, cannot leave The Crash. Sacrifices himself willingly when it's revealed he is the Lich's phylactery, passing his Oath to Yelena before Tycho executes him.

---

## Campaign 3 Session Detail

Serves as the party's guide and protector across the desert of The Crash, riding his camel Goby and pulling a lit dune sledge to keep the Rot at bay. Lives by self-imposed rules he repeats often ("Everything has rules"): he cannot kill, cannot lie, and cannot leave The Crash — breaking any of them would mean passing down his Oath. Cooks a healing curry for the party after a brutal sandworm fight. When Sebash the Rakshasa reveals Goroshi is the lich Nebbuzar's phylactery, Goroshi accepts it calmly, has Yelena Moller take his Oath of Remembrance, and is executed by Tycho at his own request — last seen feeding apples to Goby and asking the party to look after the camel.

His death frees Tirgad, the undead dragon who had been bound as Nebbuzar's undead court since his betrayal centuries ago.
