# Logan

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire, CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World

---

## Overview

Benji's apprentice turned betrayer. Used blood magic experiments on Benji's body and held a centuries-long vendetta. Eventually bound as a vampiric shadow lord in Myka's Keep. Destroyed by a wish (cast via Myka using one of Benji's letters: "I wish you would disintegrate into nothing"). Petty, bitter, and consumed by resentment even in undeath.

---

## Campaign 2 Session Detail

Cursed Zeth Nogoth — his own former student, unable to learn blood magic — into a being of shadow ("Shy-dar") and sealed him away for roughly three centuries as punishment for trying to save the land.

---

## Campaign 3 Session Detail

*⚠️ Continuity note: Logan was previously destroyed outright by Myka's wish in Campaign 2 ("I wish you would disintegrate into nothing"). The Campaign 3 notes show his spirit resurfacing, possessing/animating Benji's recovered body in the mausoleum beneath Shaidar's Rest — apparently his true goal was always to destroy Benji as an Oathstone, not to hold the Keep. Flagging this rather than assuming it contradicts his earlier "destruction," since spirits and undeath are established as survivable in this setting (see Mortalus, Tirgad).*

Possesses Benji's body in the mausoleum and is fought again by the party (aided by Benji's Time Stop). This time he's trapped for good in a binding jar purchased from Sunny and Moony for 15,000 gold — the same type of jar Logan once used to imprison Zeth Nogoth/Veth.
