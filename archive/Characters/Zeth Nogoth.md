# Zeth Nogoth

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

Protector of shadows. An apprentice of Benji's apprentice (Logan) who mastered shadow magic after being unable to learn blood magic. Cursed into a being of shadow ("Shy-dar") by Logan as punishment for trying to save the land, and sealed away for roughly three centuries. When freed by Enki, immediately dedicates himself to defending Myka's Keep, too ashamed of his long imprisonment to face Rynn. Combines shadow magic and nature magic to make the very trees fight back, and is able to kill multiple blood mages outright with shadow tentacles during the siege of the Keep.

**⚠️ Possible duplicate:** [Veth (Shidar)](../NPCs/Veth.md) — a separate background-character entry from the compendium — describes an almost identical figure: a shadow swordsman defending Myka in Shaidar's Rest, recommended as a trainer for Star, who kills three blood mages with shadow tentacles during a siege. "Shidar" and "Shy-dar" both appear to derive from the same deep-speech word for "shadows." These may well be the same character recorded under two names from two source passages — flagging rather than merging until confirmed.

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Killed roughly 2,000 years later by a new figure who rises to power in Shaidar's Rest calling himself "the Pridemaster," amid a Tiefling-vs-Leonin caste conflict following the city's flooding and the resurgence of blood magic. See the title-conflict flag on [Tycho's file](../../Characters/Tycho.md), since Tycho also carries the title "the Pridemaster" from Campaigns 1–2.

---

## Campaign 3 Session Detail

During the blood magic riot in Shaidar's Rest, transforms fully into his shadow-monstrosity form — described as barely holding together even in that state — and single-handedly kills several blood mages with shadow tentacles before the party arrives. Goes unresponsive to Myka and Seraph's messages right before the riot breaks out, which the party reads (correctly) as an emergency. Afterward, executes the majority of the surviving blood-magic prisoners alongside Tin of Lost Hope.
