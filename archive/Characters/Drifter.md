# Drifter

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

**CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire:**

A friendly Greatshell who wanders between worlds looking for an end to all conflict. Has two shadows (noticeable only on a high Perception check) — implying he is more than he presents. Carries a Live Well for keeping fish alive. Not from Mythrir but deeply invested in its survival. Fights alongside Carp to defend the Frost Shepherds' keep during the Beyond assault (mutated riders on anteater-headed mounts).
