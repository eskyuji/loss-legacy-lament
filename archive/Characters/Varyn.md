# Varyn

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

A stitchwork fighter assembled from the limbs of multiple races — a catfolk arm here, a lizardfolk arm there. Charged by Tin to re-found the Moa. Earnest, serious, and deeply aware of the weight of history. Carries a severed head of Feit as a greeting gift.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Introduces his reformed-Moa party to the Frost Shepherds during the downtime sequence, presenting the severed head of one of Feit's nine simultaneous selves as an opening gift — a gesture equal parts diplomatic and unsettling, given how many other versions of Feit remain active.
