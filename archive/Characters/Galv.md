# Galv

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

A bald Truthspeaker with burnt skin and splotchy coloring. Part of a small organization of beings who feel every pain they've ever experienced continuously. Named after the symbol of their group. Has the ability to teleport using powerful magical items as fuel. Works under Nakki. Becomes a crucial ally when Shaidar's Rest falls to darkness.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Fuels his greatest teleportation of the campaign using the imprisoned soul of Madam Lennette, on loan from Meeka — the same screaming rag doll documented in her NPC entry. Works directly under Nakki throughout the Truthspeakers' introduction arc and the fall of Shaidar's Rest.
