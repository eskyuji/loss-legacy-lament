# Tirgad

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World, CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

**CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World:**

An undead dragon who rose to dominance after Goroshi's death freed him. In life he was King of the northern desert, betrayed by his court wizard (Goroshi). In undeath he commands the mindless undead hordes by sheer force of will. Can take the form of an average Rhox for diplomatic conversations. Offers the party a substantial haul of magic loot — a Manual of Stone Golems, a Ring of Earth Elemental Command, Mariner's Ringmail, Hayward's Handy Haversack, a Portable Hole, three Quaal's Feather Tokens, a Chime of Opening, Horseshoes of a Zephyr, an unidentified 9th-level spell scroll, a Lantern of Revealing, and an 8th-level scroll of Abi-Dalzim's Horrid Wilting — in exchange for retrieving bloodstones from the Scarlet Peaks for The Magus. Open to diplomacy with The Crash eventually, but wants to rebuild his strength first.

**CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire:**

An undead dragon, the most powerful undead remaining after the necropolis fell. In life he was betrayed by Goroshi, his own court wizard. In undeath he commands the mindless horde by sheer force of will. Agrees to diplomacy when given the chance. Gifts the party a haversack full of legendary items in exchange for a promise to retrieve bloodstones.
