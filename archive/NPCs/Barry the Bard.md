# Barry the Bard

**Type:** Background NPC
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A small halfling bartender at the Coat Hanger in Salamada, carrying a Mocking Bird guild symbol. Has a drunken friend who keeps taking out debts in his name. Resurfaces later at the Bar With No Name, again working off a debt — this time taken out by Barry the Parrot.

*Possible naming echo of "Barry," the late barkeep at the Cat's Pajamas referenced in Campaign 2's Thelonius file — kept as a separate, unrelated character given the different era, location, and species (halfling vs. unspecified) rather than merged.*
