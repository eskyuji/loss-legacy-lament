# Sypt / Grail

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

Two people sharing one body — a Triton. If the arms are crossed it's Sypt; if the arms are at the sides it's Grail. They are aware of each other and don't appreciate being called by the wrong name. Don't put their names in strangers' books.
