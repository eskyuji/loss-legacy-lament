# Xanathar

**Type:** Major NPC / Moa
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A Moa residing in Gul'Malar, where he runs a thieves' guild that bears his own name, The Xanathar. Knows the party killed Corthus the Fallen Star and speaks with them about it directly, revealing that the illithids fled to the Underdark to escape horrors unleashed by an incident at Lake Urathil. Pelunar and Salazar the bugbear (also a Moa) could corroborate his account of Underdark history.
