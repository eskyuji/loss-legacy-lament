# Rico

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

A rat boy who runs errands for the Emperor out of the Scrapyard. Small, quick, and reliable. One of the first to report that snow was actually falling on Shaidar's Rest — confirming the strange omen that kicks the campaign into motion.
