# The Pridemaster (Campaign 4)

**Type:** Background Character / Antagonist
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

A new figure who rises to power in Shaidar's Rest roughly 2,000 years after the original Frost Shepherds' era, during the Quiet Year covered in Campaign 4's Session 0 lore. Emerges amid a Tiefling-vs-Leonin caste conflict following the city's flooding and the resurgence of blood magic, and personally kills Zeth Nogoth on his way to claiming the title.

*⚠️ Title conflict: "the Pridemaster" is also the title held by [Tycho](../../NPCs/Tycho) across Campaigns 1–2. Whether this Campaign 4 figure is a usurper claiming an old, vacated title, the title simply recurring as an office passed down in Shaidar's Rest, or something else entirely has not been confirmed in session. Filed as a separate entry rather than merged into Tycho's file, since the raw notes treat him as a distinct character — flagging the name collision rather than resolving it.*
