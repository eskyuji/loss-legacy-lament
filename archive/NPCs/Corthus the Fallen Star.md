# Corthus the Fallen Star

**Type:** Major NPC / Moa (deceased)
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

"The One True Moa" — a thirty-foot-tall spider-woman, encountered in the desert. Confirmed by Xanathar to have genuinely been a Moa. Defeated by the party, who take her Moa Coin afterward — the same coin Paavu carries as confirmation of his new status as a Moa.

*Spelling note: the Discord lore channel spells this name "Quorsys" (citation needed, per the DM's own note) rather than "Corthus" — both kept on file, with "Corthus" as the primary filename since it matches the player's raw notes.*
