# Silkworm

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn, CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A small goblin wearing an Oni mask, carrying a bird pendant. The current holder of the title Silkworm — the leader of the Thieves' Guild, the Crows. Operates in Empyr, using guild money to care for the people that the city's corrupt leadership ignores. Wants to fully reform the Crows and restore the old guild order. Charges the party one hundred platinum per person to smuggle them into the city.

*The Campaign 4 framing of "the current holder of the title" is confirmed and explained by the Campaign 1 raw notes below: the original Silkworm dies in that campaign, establishing the name as a passed-down title rather than one person's whole identity.*

---

## Campaign 1 Session Detail

The original known holder of the title: a former assassin for King Phresee, ruler of the human lands above the Underdark. Tells the party to seek him out at the Poisoned Dagger tavern in Gol'talum. A guard named Billy separately claims a man in a gold cloak named Silkworm slaughtered forty guards single-handed — Billy seems to believe it, though the notes leave open whether it was really him. Ultimately cut into nine pieces and killed in front of the Canaries' headquarters by a masked man, presumed to be one of the Moa.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Smuggles the party into Empyr ahead of the Geno Atul confrontation at the city's climax, charging the standard hundred-platinum-per-person toll.
