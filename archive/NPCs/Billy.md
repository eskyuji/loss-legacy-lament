# Billy

**Type:** Background NPC
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A human guard at the first gate into the Underdark. Later, as the guard in Gol'talum, tells the party that a man in a gold cloak named Silkworm slaughtered forty guards single-handed — he seems to believe it, though the party isn't sure it was really Silkworm.
