# Iphitus

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire, CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

An avatar of The Watcher and champion of light. One of the original Moa. The Order of Iphitus — the healer faction — is founded in his name.

---

## Campaign 1 Session Detail

Recognized by Ruby the Glass Eye while staying at the Four Flumphs in Norathin — he flees rather than face her, suggesting an unresolved history between them predating this campaign. Later present when the party kills Lehinar (also known as Uriel), a Moa who demanded they grovel before the Beyond's divine power; Iphitus claims her body afterward.
