# Faro the Halfling

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

The greatest grower in the world — the only person capable of planting Heart Trees, which can retain the memories of what land used to be. Left the Council of Varaneth when things got complicated. Thelonius considers his absence a genuine loss.
