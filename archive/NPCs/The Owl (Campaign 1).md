# The Owl (Campaign 1)

**Type:** Major NPC
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A talking owl, met by Paavu and Ryx on Day 1 of the campaign timeline — the earliest-dated event on record for this era. Warns the party that the Maw will fall, tells them to seek out Silkworm, and announces that a tear in reality has happened — death is coming to the Underdark.

The Discord lore channel defines "the Owls" as one of ten Underdark guilds: "insane scribes of the supernatural in the Underdark." That gives this figure a concrete in-world affiliation, distinct from — though possibly an early ancestor of — the "Owls" who recur as agents of fate in Campaigns 2 and 4 (see [Locations/Hall of the Owls.md](../../Locations/Hall%20of%20the%20Owls) and the Tower of the Owls). Whether the guild of scribes and the cosmic agents of fate are meant to be the same order across thousands of years, or just a name reused twice, remains unconfirmed — flagging the connection rather than asserting it.
