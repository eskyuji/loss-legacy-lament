# Black Blood of Yag'saruf

**Type:** Entity / possible Beyond-aligned god
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

The true form Ruby the Glass Eye reveals when the party damages her badly enough — described in the Discord lore channel simply as "a terrifying ooze monster from beyond the world." Per the player's raw notes, it claims to be the real God of Death and states that Mythrir is already within the Beyond's grasp. Falls to a revived, "uncaged" Yannick, who takes Ruby's glass eye afterward.

*⚠️ Possible conflict: [[Mortalus (Mort)]] is the established God of Death across Campaigns 2 and onward. This entity's competing claim to that title is left unresolved by the raw notes — flagging rather than assuming either is lying.*
