# The Guilds of the Underdark

**Type:** Lore / faction-class entry
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

Per the Discord lore channel, the Underdark's political structure (as of Campaign 1) is organized around ten named guilds, each themed after a bird:

| Guild | Role |
|---|---|
| The Hawks | Mercenary corps — led by Vice Conroy. |
| The Condors | Adventurers' guild. |
| The Whippoorwills | Loose alliance of clerics. |
| The Crows | Thieves and spies — the guild whose leadership title "Silkworm" passes between successive holders. |
| The Canaries | Miners' guild — de facto led by Ruby the Glass Eye. |
| The Gulls | Sailors of the underground seas. |
| The Parrots | Pirates. |
| The Owls | Insane scribes of the supernatural in the Underdark — see [The Owl (Campaign 1)](../NPCs/The Owl (Campaign 1)) for the possible/unconfirmed connection to the "Owls" of later campaigns. |
| The Mockingbirds | Bard college — Barry the Bard carries a Mocking Bird guild symbol. |
| The Moa | A dead, ancient guild — though Campaign 1 finds it very much active, if scattered and secretive; see the many individual Moa entries (Paavu, Ryx, Behelor, Lehinar, Xanathar, Corthus the Fallen Star, and others). |

---

## Notes

*This recontextualizes the player's raw notes, which describe the Canaries and Crows mainly through their individual leaders (Ruby, Silkworm) rather than as part of a complete ten-guild system. No conflict — just a fuller picture from a more authoritative source.*
