# Lake Urathil

**Type:** Location (Underdark)
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

## Overview

The destination the party heads toward at the start of this campaign. Later revealed by Xanathar to be the site of an incident that unleashed horrors which drove the illithids to flee into the Underdark in the first place.
