# Empyr

**Type:** City
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

A corrupt city under leadership the party never comes to trust, contested in the shadows by the Thieves' Guild (the Crows), currently led by the goblin Silkworm. The party is smuggled in by Silkworm for a hundred platinum per person. A rot farm tended by the Greatshell Myconid guardian Rotguardian Ixtar sits nearby. The site of the campaign's climactic confrontation with Geno Atul — the Ghost of the Third Moon — in the ruins of Zaani's (the Emperor's) old throne room, interrupted mid-fight by a note and a swarm of paper owls sent by Fate.
