# Myka's Keep

**Type:** Fortress / domain  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

A fortress watched over by Rynn, a centaur who plucked out his own eyes rather than see what lurked inside, for three hundred years. For centuries it was secretly under the sway of Logan, Benji's betrayer-apprentice, who was eventually bound here as a vampiric shadow lord. Defended by Zeth Nogoth (possibly the same figure recorded elsewhere as Veth/Shidar — see his file), who combines shadow and nature magic to make the trees themselves fight back, and who killed several blood mages outright with shadow tentacles during a siege.

Logan's reign over the Keep ended when Myka destroyed him with a wish, drawn from one of Benji's letters.
