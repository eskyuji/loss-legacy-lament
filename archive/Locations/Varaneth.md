# Varaneth

**Type:** City  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

A capital of the Eternal Empire and the campaign's central hub city. Home to the Council of Varaneth, which Faro the Halfling left when its politics became too complicated. Site of an orphanage, Little Hope, founded by Enki Steelbane and Myka. Hosts the annual Festival of Lights, during which Thelonius always returns to stay at the Cat's Pajamas.

---

## Notable Locations & Businesses

- **The Cat's Pajamas** — an inn run day-to-day by Laylah and her husband Gary, staffed in part by the Warforged 773 Philosophical Directory. Thelonius holds court here and considers it a second home.
- **Vincent's Vitals and Vittles** — a shop run by a Yuan-Ti named Vincent who deals in vitality rather than gold; Enki traded 30 maximum hit points here to have a party member's staff enchanted.
- **Wesley Valentine's jewelry shop** — made pieces for Myka, and sold Abacus his Ring of Spell Storing.
- **Goods & Glorious Gains** — an armorsmith that outfitted Myka.
- **Little Hope** — orphanage founded by Enki and Myka.
