# Tower of the Third Moon

**Type:** Landmark / dungeon
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

Reached via Aleer's airship, the Heracles. Aleer dies here at the hands of a corrupted Seraph; in the aftermath, Bas Tetran XXIV and Netiri touch Aleer's divine blood, and Bas becomes "the Godblooded." Later, Teyou Zhiang (the World Crafter) delivers the campaign's central cosmological lore-dump in connection with this location — Feit as "the Dark Door," Orthel as Teyou's shed madness usurped by Feit, and True Ice as a weapon against him.
