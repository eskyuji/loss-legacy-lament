# The Grand Magics

**Type:** Lore / artifact-class entry — world-changing spells
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A set of 10th-level-and-above spells, each tied to a specific Moa, offered to the party as options for dealing with the Beyond/Typhlo-Urath crisis (per the player's raw notes: "All the Moa have 10+ level spells which are world-changing. We have to decide which one we would want to go through with."). The Discord lore channel provides the full list:

| Spell | School | Level | Tied to |
|---|---|---|---|
| Salazar's Mirrored Bastion | Abjuration | 11th | [[Salazar]] |
| The Xanathar's Pocket Dimension | Conjuration | 10th | [[Xanathar]] |
| Kytaar's Sands of Time | Divination | 10th | [[Kitar the Sphynx]] (spelling variant "Kytaar") |
| The Nameless' Fungal Recruitment | Enchantment | 10th | [[The Nameless One (Moa)]] |
| Behelor's Breath of the Mountain | Evocation | 10th | [[Behelor]] |
| Skittarak's Killing Wind X | Necromancy | 11th | Possibly [[Sitherak the Alhoun]] (spelling variant) |
| Polunaar's Move Reality | Transmutation | 11th | Possibly "Pelunar," referenced in the player's raw notes as a Moa who could corroborate Xanathar's account |
| Blood of the Moa | ???? | 12th | Unattributed — name and owner left blank in the source ("????") |

## Notes

*Several of these spell names use spelling variants of established Moa names (Kytaar/Kitar, Skittarak/Sitherak, Polunaar/Pelunar) — kept as-is rather than normalized, since it's unclear which spelling (if either) is "correct." Mechanical effects of each spell are not detailed in the source material.*
