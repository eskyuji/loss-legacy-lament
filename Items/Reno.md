## Fate (confirmed Campaign 5, Session 1)

At some point between Campaign 4 and Campaign 5, Reno was melted down and reforged into a bismuth breastplate for Emp, the Emperor (Za'a-ni) — see [The Emperor (Za'a-ni)](../NPCs/The Emperor (Za'a-ni)). No longer exists in its original sword form.

---

## Origin (confirmed Campaign 5, Session 2)

Thelonius reveals that this blade was originally Tyn's own friend — a person — transformed into a weapon at the moment Tyn became an Oathstone (see [Oathstones](Oathstones) and [Tyn](../NPCs/Tyn)). What happened to that sentience or identity when the blade was later melted down into Emp's breastplate is not addressed in session — flagging the tension rather than resolving it.

*⚠️ Naming note: the Session 1 transcript calls the giver "Tyn," and Session 2's transcript renders the sword's own name as "Rynn" at one point (Bas himself is visibly unsure — "Am I remembering that name right? Rin?"). Both are treated here as the same "Tyn"/"Tin" and the same "Reno"/"Rynn" already established elsewhere on file — see the naming note on [Tyn](../NPCs/Tyn).*
