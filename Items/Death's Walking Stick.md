# Death's Walking Stick (a.k.a. Death's Scythe)

**Type:** Scythe (relic), attuned  
**Owner:** Originally Mortalus (Mort); passed to Tycho  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire, CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World

## Item Table

| Item | 5e Description | Visual Adaptation |
|------|---------------|-------------------|
| Death's Walking Stick / Death's Scythe | Mortalus's scythe, capable of opening teleportation rifts — confirmed in Campaign 3 to be able to tear open portals to other realms/planes when used against the undead. By Campaign 3 it has changed in form: lighter, more solid, no longer the energy-scythe look it had as Mort's weapon — now a plain, heavy hunk of dark steel.
