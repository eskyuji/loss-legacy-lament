# Moa Coin

**Type:** Artifact / status token
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## 5e Description / Visual Adaptation

| Aspect | Detail |
|---|---|
| Mechanical function | Not yet established — appears to function as proof of Moa status rather than a confirmed magic item with stated effects. |
| Visual description | A coin carried by each Moa; Paavu takes Corthus the Fallen Star's coin after defeating her, and is afterward recognized as a Moa himself. A separate half-elf in Gul'Malar carries an unrelated guild coin bearing an eyeball. |

---

## Overview

Tied to the Scales of Balance — touching the Scales is what triggers Paavu to hear Vice Conroy's voice. Each of the nine (or more) Moa are implied to hold one. Functions in the campaign as both a personal token and a marker of legitimacy among the Moa.
