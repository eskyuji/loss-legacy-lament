# Aleer

**Type:** Minor NPC  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn, CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

An Aarakocra who served as the party's commanding officer in the Eternal Empire campaign. Carries a single drop of divine blood — the God's blood — that has kept him alive for a millennium and his descendants for longer. Captains the Heracles, the last remaining ship of the Eternal Empire. Dies in Campaign 4 when Seraph, now a twisted abomination, punches a softball-sized hole through his chest. His God-blood, touched by Bas and Netiri, transforms Bas into the Godblooded.

*⚠️ Note: the original compendium blurb places his death in "Campaign 1," but per the Campaign 4 raw notes ("Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn"), this death occurs aboard the Heracles during the Tower of the Third Moon arc — i.e., Campaign 4. Corrected above; flagging the relabel rather than silently overwriting it.*

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Captains the Heracles on the journey to the Tower of the Third Moon. Dies there at Seraph's hand (Seraph corrupted/twisted at the time). His divine God-blood — touched by Bas Tetran and Netiri in the aftermath — is what transforms Bas into "the Godblooded."
