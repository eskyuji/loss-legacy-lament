# The Yuan-Ti

*(of Ryxspine Lake)*

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

Ancient, serpentine, and operating out of a shack near Ryxspine Lake. Hunts Feit across all of time and space. Pure hatred for Feit is the first genuine emotion the party sees from him. Makes deals in exchange for pieces of people — a sliver of December's fighting spirit, years of Bas's life. Vanishes the moment the deal is made, shack and all.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Trades Bas Tetran XXIV years of his own life for the dagger that becomes the Coiled Viper, attached to Bas's blade Yesiah. Vanishes — shack and all — the moment the deal is sealed.
