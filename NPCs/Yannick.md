# Yannick

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire, CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

Called "the blade in the dark." Mort's champion. Also known as One-Eyed Jack the lesser divinity — a figure who began as a thief, became a hero, then a legend, then a god of death and service. Tyra, the Sword of Typhlo, has sworn to kill him for his role in the death of the Twin Gods.

*Confirms a connection already anticipated in the Campaign 4 file [NPCs/One-Eyed Jack.md](../Characters/One-Eyed%20Jack.md), which notes its own One-Eyed Jack "is named for the divine figure... though this Jack appears to be just a regular, chaotic guy." This file establishes that divine figure as Yannick.*

---

## Campaign 1 Session Detail

Dispatched early (Day 12) to scout the Owls. Rejoins the party near the Hall of the Owls (Day 96). His truesight reveals that a water genasi encountered in the town of Typhlo is in fact a "Blood of the Beyond." His role in "the death of the Twin Gods" — presumably Typhlo and Urath, the underdark gods whose mutual battle created the Underdark itself — is referenced elsewhere as the reason Tyra hunts him, though the notes don't show that death occurring directly in this campaign's events.

At the climax of the Ruby the Glass Eye confrontation (Day 105), he's caught trying to sneak up on her and has his throat slit — but Mort'Talus (Mortalus in disguise) offers him a second chance at life in exchange for taking over his pact. Revived and "uncaged," Yannick then defeats Ruby's true ooze form and takes her glass eye, an act the lore channel describes as starting him "down a long path" — presumably the beginning of his rise from mortal thief to the lesser divinity later known as One-Eyed Jack.
