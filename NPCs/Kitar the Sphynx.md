# Kitar the Sphynx

**Type:** Major NPC / Moa
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A Moa who has "fallen to the wayside" and no longer serves balance. Resides in something called "the turn," described as the crux of the timeline. Can hurl people back to the moment Typhlo and Urath first battled, theoretically allowing the conflict to be fixed before it starts. Mortalus says he is able to warp time — something not even the gods can do.
