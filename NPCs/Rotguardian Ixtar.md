A massive Greatshell Myconid guardian at the rot farm near Empyr. Covered in moss, grass, and saplings growing from his shell. Carries a log as a staff. The ground shakes when he walks. Immediately defers all resources to Netiri when she identifies herself as a Rot Guardian.
