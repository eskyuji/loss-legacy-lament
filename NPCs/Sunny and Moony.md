# Sunny and Moony

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

The greatest relic hunters in the known and unknown world. Sunny is a black dog; Moony is a white Persian cat. Both are cursed like Thelonius — stuck in animal form. Work together as a pair and charge aggressively for their services. Located the Oathstone of Eternity in the mausoleum beneath Shaidar's Rest.
