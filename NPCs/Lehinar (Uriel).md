# Lehinar (Uriel)

**Type:** Major NPC / Moa (deceased)
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A Moa, confirmed Aasimar per the Discord lore channel (4/8/19 DM post: "Lehinar- Aasimar"), and Behelor's rival — Behelor sets the party up to defeat him as a show of good faith. Also referred to as Uriel in the player's raw notes. Tells the party to grovel and beg for help, insisting mere mortals cannot stop the divine power of the Beyond; the party kills him after he insults them, taking his Fiery Crown. Iphitus claims the body afterward.

*⚠️ Pronoun inconsistency: the Discord lore timeline refers to Lehinar as male ("his rival," "his Fiery Crown"), while the player's raw notes use "her" throughout ("kill her," "Lehinar... her"). Keeping both sources on file rather than silently picking one. The earlier ambiguous pairing with [[Behelor]] in the player notes ("Behelor / Lehinar (?) — Aasimar/Tiefling Moa") is resolved by the same DM post: Behelor is the Tiefling, Lehinar is the Aasimar — two distinct Moa, not one uncertain reference.*
