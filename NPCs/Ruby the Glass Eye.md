# Ruby the Glass Eye

**Type:** Major NPC / Antagonist
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A 5'5" red-tinged tiefling woman who wears sunglasses and looks more human than the average tiefling. One of her eyes is a literal ruby; her real eye underneath is red. De facto leader of the Canaries, a guild that rivals the Crows/Silkworm's organization. Knows three of the nine Moa locations and is aware of Silkworm's "quest." Recognizes Iphitus on sight in the Four Flumphs at Norathin — he flees rather than face her, implying history between them. Attacks Behelor (revealed to be Baphomet) after the party wounds her.

*⚠️ See [[Behelor]] for the contradiction her scene with "Baphomet" raises.*

---

## Campaign 1 Session Detail

De facto leader of the Canaries, the Underdark's miners' guild (see [[The Guilds of the Underdark]]). Charges the party with killing a Moa (Day 72), around the time the original Silkworm turns up dead. Confronts the party again at Norathin after Behelor is struck by an arrow (Day 105) — Yannick is caught trying to sneak up on her and has his throat slit for it. When the party damages her further, she transforms into the **Black Blood of Yag'saruf** — see that entry, "a terrifying ooze monster from beyond the world" — and claims to be the true God of Death, declaring that Mythrir is already in its grasp. She falls to a revived, "uncaged" Yannick, who takes her glass eye afterward.
