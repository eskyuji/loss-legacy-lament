# Master Tinkerer Morrin

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

Never appears directly but is referenced with reverence. A great donor to the Greatshell orphanages alongside Magerna. The Emperor credits Morrin's tools as the foundation of everything he builds. A ghost of craft and legacy that haunts the Emperor's sense of purpose.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Referenced again by both the Emperor and Magerna in the same conversation, reinforcing his role as a shared touchstone for both characters despite never appearing directly.
