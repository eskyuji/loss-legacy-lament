# Iyo

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

A Wilden — a tree-like plant person — and member of Varyn's Moa. Grows silver roses from their bark. Actively heals and tends to the other members of the group mid-battle. Deeply attuned to the natural cycle and immediately recognizes Netiri's ancient mushroom as something dangerous and hungry.
