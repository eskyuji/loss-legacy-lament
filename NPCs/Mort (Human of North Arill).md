# Mort (Human of North Arill) — RETRACTED, see Mortalus (Mort)

**Type:** Correction record
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Correction

This file originally treated "Mort" — a human ally in North Arill who helps clear an illithid infestation and keeps two lesser demons in his charge — as a separate, unrelated character from the established death god Mortalus, based only on the player's sparse raw notes.

The Discord lore channel's DM-authored timeline confirms this was wrong: this "Mort" travels with the party from Day 48 (the North Arill illithid clearing) onward, and on Day 105 reveals himself to be the god of death, Mor'Talus — the same figure established elsewhere as Mortalus. He was disguised, not a separate person. All content has been moved to [[Mortalus (Mort)]] — see that file's Campaign 1 Session Detail for the full account. Keeping this file only as a record of the correction rather than deleting it outright.
