# Nakki (Akki Nakki)

**Type:** Major NPC  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

**CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire:**

A red goblin cursed with the Curse of Truth — she cannot lie even when she doesn't know the answer. Has scaly skin, a turtle shell on her back, and eyes fully glazed over white. Appears throughout history as a mysterious recurring figure who travels via teleporting vines. Leads the Truthspeakers. Has been around long enough to personally know every major faction in Mythrir's history, and supplies the party with deep lore on the Bringers, the gods, and the Moa. Deeply complicated — her meddling always has unintended consequences even when her intentions are good.
