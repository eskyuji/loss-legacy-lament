# Salazar

**Type:** Background NPC / Moa
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

An eight-foot bugbear serving as Ruby the Glass Eye's guard. Sent by Ruby to retrieve Maryeth, who never showed up. Later revealed to apparently be a Moa himself — Xanathar suggests Salazar could corroborate his account of the Underdark's history.
