# Myhama

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

The healer of the Truthspeakers. A woman with blotchy skin found gravely injured when Galv arrives with his people. Described as the one who finds new Truthspeakers and teaches them their capabilities. It's been five or six years since she found the last one before Desiree.
