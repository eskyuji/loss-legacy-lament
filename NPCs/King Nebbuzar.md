# King Nebbuzar

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

An ancient lich — the King of a pre-Empire desert kingdom called Nebbuzar. Stored part of his soul elsewhere to survive the blood moon. Believed the coming darkness and rot were his machinations finally coming to fruition. Dismissed by the party; his necropolis crashes to the ground when Goroshi is killed.
