# The Blood Magic Orphan

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

## Overview

A nameless child driven out of Shaidar's Rest when blood magic was outlawed. Left the city in search of power and revenge, traveled to the Scarlet Peaks, and revived Monsieur Pierre. A potential villain origin story mentioned but never resolved.
