# Kythrazi

**Type:** Major NPC / God
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A god in the form of a two-headed bird person, patron of crafting — confirmed by the Discord lore channel. Per Mortalus, rose to godhood rather than being born into it — nearly as old as Aerynath. A statue base near Aerynath's dragon statue bears Kythrazi's name and tail. The Grand Magic "Kytaar's Sands of Time" (10th-level Divination, per the lore channel's spell list) is likely tied to Kitar the Sphynx rather than to Kythrazi despite the similar spelling — see [[Kitar the Sphynx]].

*⚠️ Spelling variant: also written "Kithrazi" within the same raw notes, and "Kithrazi" is referenced separately in the existing [Locations/Hall of the Owls.md](../Locations/Hall%20of%20the%20Owls.md) and the Campaign 2 Overall Summary as one of several figures who "have fallen" defending Varaneth — consistent with this entity's status as a god from this earlier era. No existing dedicated file previously existed for this name; this is a new entry, not a merge with an established conflicting file.*
