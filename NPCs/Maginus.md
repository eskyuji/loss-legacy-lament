# Maginus

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

Tycho's father. Difficult, dismissive, and condescending toward his son. Works as the Empire's primary negotiator to Varaneth. Bailey uses her emotional metal magic on him to keep him manageable. Tycho insults him at dinner and is magically forced by his mother to apologize.
