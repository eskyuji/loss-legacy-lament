# Star

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

Tycho's squire and one of the casualties of the blood magic assault on Shaidar's Rest in Campaign 3\. A young catfolk who, in the downtime between campaigns, set off to build relations with the druids of the northern forest in an attempt to make Tycho proud. Killed in the siege. Tycho's grief over Star's death marks one of his darkest moments.
