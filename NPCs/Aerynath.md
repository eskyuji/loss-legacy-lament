# Aerynath

**Type:** Major NPC / God
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A massive nine-headed dragon god, ancestor of both Tiamat and Bahamut, metallic and chromatic. Confirmed by the Discord lore channel as the Patron of Life, who often takes the form of a beggar. A statue near Kythrazi depicts him, tail wrapped around its base. Appears in person as the beggar in the Maw, showing the party a set of scales depicting Typhlo and Urath — unbalanced.

*⚠️ Naming overlap: the existing Character Index lists "Aerynth" as a hero who fell defending Varaneth during Campaign 2's three-week siege aftermath. Given the spelling difference (Aerynth vs. Aerynath) and the gap between "a fallen hero" and "a massive nine-headed dragon god," this may be the same figure remembered imperfectly across millennia, or two distinct entities. Flagging rather than merging.*
