# Paavu

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire, CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A warlord and one of the original Moa. Also exists in the Hall of the Owls in both corporeal and spirit form.

---

## Campaign 1 Session Detail

Becomes "the newest Moa" over the course of this campaign — confirmed by Corthus the Fallen Star, who recognizes him as such and whose Moa Coin he takes after she's defeated. Fights (and nearly loses to) a dragon turtle in a fight club in Gol'talum, earning exactly one fan: a halfling named Pharaoh. Sent to Addie's Acquisitions by a potion seller named Severus to learn more about the Xanathar thieves' guild. Later hears the voice of Vice Conroy, leader of "the Hawks," upon touching the Scales of Balance. Sought out by Behelor in Norathin once his new status as a Moa becomes known.
