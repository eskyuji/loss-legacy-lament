# Sebash

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

A Rakshasa who approached the party in the desert during a sandstorm, disguising himself as a tiger scavenger. Servant of the lich Nebbuzar, sent to stop the party from sieging the necropolis. Mind-controlled by Seraph after attempting to mind-control Tycho. Revealed that Goroshi was the lich's phylactery before biting his own tongue and bleeding out.
