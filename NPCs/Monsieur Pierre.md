# Monsieur Pierre

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

The prime consort of Madam Lennette — the Scarlet Queen of Blood Magic. Revived by a desperate blood magic orphan after centuries of death. Masterminded a new Crimson Winter, infiltrating Shaidar's Rest with blood mages disguised as healers. Eventually goes missing mid-campaign, his plans left unfinished.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Tied into the blood magic resurgence that floods Shaidar's Rest during the Quiet Year and the rise of the new Pridemaster. His revival arc runs in parallel to the doll given by Meeka in the bazaar scene.
