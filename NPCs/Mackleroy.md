# Mackleroy

**Type:** Background NPC
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A dwarf from North Arill who gives the party the key to his shop and asks for a painting back from it. Friends with Art, owner of the Cracked Flask in the Maw of Arrath — mentioning his name there gets the party a free room.
