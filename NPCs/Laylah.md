# Laylah

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

An innkeeper's helper at the Cat's Pajamas in Varaneth. Sees Thelonius every year during the Festival of Lights and always saves a room for him. Her husband Gary manages the inn the rest of the year.
