# Bailey

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

Tycho's mother. Ended up in Varaneth and became Thelonius' apprentice, learning emotional metal magic — specifically the kind that dulls and enhances feelings. Uses her powers to keep Tycho's difficult father Maginus in check. Reunited with Tycho at a dinner that briefly feels like a normal family.
