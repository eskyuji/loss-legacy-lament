# Madam Lennette

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

Never appears in the flesh — her soul is imprisoned inside a screaming rag doll kept by Meeka. Was the Queen of all Blood Magic users and the most powerful hemomancer in Mythrir's history. So dangerous that even in doll form she serves as a powerful magical fuel source. Her spirit powers Galv's greatest teleportation.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Meeka gives the doll to Galv during the bazaar scene as fuel for his greatest teleportation of the campaign.
