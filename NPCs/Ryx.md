# Ryx

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire, CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A kobold who became a dragon. One of the original Moa. Her corpse and living body both exist simultaneously in the Hall of the Owls. Her ring — the Ring of Ryx — is a key to the Hall.

---

## Campaign 1 Session Detail

Present with the party during the Underdark expedition predating her ascension to dragonhood and Moa status — meets the talking Owl alongside Paavu on Day 1, and helps assemble the adventuring party by Day 8. Notably absent for both Paavu's fight club bout in Gol'talum and Billy the guard's account of Silkworm slaughtering forty guards single-handed. Persuades a figure named Sorgog to recruit two thousand Underdark small-folk and eighty dragonborn to the party's cause, though a dragon recruit proves out of reach through that channel.

At Gul'Malar (Day 86), Xanathar gifts the party a dragon called Xixxern (spelling uncertain) specifically so Ryx can "claim vengeance" — implying unresolved history between them. Given Ryx's eventual fate as "a kobold who became a dragon," this encounter is a strong candidate for the origin of that transformation, though the raw material doesn't confirm the connection outright.
