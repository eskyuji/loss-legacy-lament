## Overview

The Ghost of the Third Moon. An entity of godlike power who manifests in the ruins of Zaani's old throne room — sitting on the throne as a leonin skeleton with a bismuth dagger through its chest. Claims to be the consequences of Zaani's sins and the spirit of the third moon. Interrupted mid-battle by a swarm of paper owls sent by Fate, who refuses to let the party lose another piece from the board.
