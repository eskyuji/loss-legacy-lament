# Behelor

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire, CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

An ancient minotaur and the last of the original Moa. Was there when the world was broken to save it. Speaks with the weight of someone who has done the unthinkable once already and will do it again if necessary.

*The species/identity question raised by the player's raw notes is resolved by the Discord lore channel's authoritative record: Behelor is confirmed to be a Tiefling (4/8/19 DM post: "Behelor- Tiefling Lehinar- Aasimar"), consistent with his "Baphomet" demon-lord reveal. This still doesn't match the "ancient minotaur" description in this file's original Overview line above — that line predates the Campaign 1 material entirely and may describe a different stage of his existence, a later transformation, or simply an inconsistency in how he was first compendium'd. Flagging the minotaur/Tiefling mismatch rather than picking one.*

---

## Campaign 1 Session Detail

Encountered in Norathin (Day 98), where the party also reunites with their old friend "Mort." Behelor sets the party up to take out his rival, the Moa Lehinar (Day 99); they comply, defeating Lehinar and taking his Fiery Crown (Day 103). When they return to Behelor with proof (Day 105), an arrow strikes him just as Ruby the Glass Eye arrives — kicking off the confrontation that ends with Ruby's death and Yannick's near-death and resurrection. Separately revealed to be "Baphomet," a Tiefling turned demon lord, whom Ruby attacks once the party has already wounded him.

Earlier in the campaign, Ruby identifies him as one of several Moa residing at Norathin who wants an audience with Paavu as the newest Moa.
