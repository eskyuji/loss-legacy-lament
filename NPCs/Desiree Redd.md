# Desiree Redd

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

December's missing daughter. Young, presumed lost but confirmed alive — not dead, not in Meeka's domain. Found imprisoned in a temple in the Maw, being used as a vessel by a version of Feit. Revealed to be a Truthspeaker, a rare power that manifested far later than normal. Has her father's fire — she was casting sunfire even while captive.
