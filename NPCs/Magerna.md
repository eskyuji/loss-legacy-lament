# Magerna

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

A 90-year-old farmer who, by the time the party finds him, has died and left a ghost behind to tend his fields. Lived entirely off the grid in a world that forgot real farming existed — his vegetables would have made him fabulously wealthy had he ever gone to market. Warm, gentle, and deeply wise. Lives on inside a ring the party carries with them. Knew Master Tinkerer Morrin personally.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

His ring-bound ghost becomes a recurring ally; his bag is found to contain the legendary Spear of Creation and Destruction.
