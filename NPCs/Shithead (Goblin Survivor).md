# Shithead (Goblin Survivor)

**Type:** Background Character
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 5 — Loss, Legacy, and Lament

---

## Overview

A young goblin — about three years old (roughly age ten in human-equivalent years) — and the sole survivor of a goblin camp of two to three dozen living under the Western Bridge in Verenath. Small, underfed, blind or scarred in one eye from an old, long-healed injury. Carries no possessions; everything he had was taken from him before the massacre. Answers only to "Shithead" — the name the rest of the camp called him — and rejects the party's offered alternative, "Lil Shysti," on first hearing it. By the end of Session 2 the party is actively looking for a better name for him, without having settled on one.

---

## Campaign 5 Session Detail

**Session 2:** Discovered by Bas, Magerna, and Meeka hiding under rubble at the edge of the goblin camp, in the aftermath of Grit's rampage (see [[Items/The Shard]]) — the camp's other roughly twenty-five members lie stacked in a heap, already in rigor, drained of color, killed by what the party recognizes as a Tychonium blade. Grit, the only camp member who had ever been kind to him, is the one who killed everyone else after acquiring "a new piece of shiny" that transformed him from small and underfed to hobgoblin-sized; a camp member named Brick tried to take the shard from Grit and was killed for it.

Flees to Bas on sight and will not be separated from him. Bas declines to use his prestige-class ability to borrow a nearby lieutenant's face once he sees the scale of the massacre, choosing instead to approach and comfort the child directly rather than pose as a town official. Cries himself to sleep in Bas's arms. Refuses to be handed off to intermediary guards, fearing further abandonment; the party — over Magerna's insistence that "it would remove any humanity from us to ignore it" — carries him directly to the Maw rather than routing him through local authorities, and delivers him personally to House Modril's orphanage there (see [[Locations/Maw of Arrath]]). Magerna gives him a straw-and-poppy bed for the journey. Transported the final 22 miles via [[Items/Liberty of the End]]'s Stars Without Number effect, held by Meeka.

See [[Sessions/Campaign 5 - Loss, Legacy, and Lament - Overall Summary]] for full scene detail.
