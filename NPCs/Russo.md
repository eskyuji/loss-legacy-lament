# Russo

**Type:** Background Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

A sixteen-year-old red goblin who serves as the Emperor's second-in-command at the Scrapyard. Young, eager, and trusted enough that the Emperor leaves him in charge of the entire operation when the party departs. Heeds the Emperor's warning to evacuate when Shaidar's Rest goes dark.
