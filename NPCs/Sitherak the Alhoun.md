# Sitherak the Alhoun (No Name)

**Type:** Major NPC
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A six-foot-tall illithid, ascended from an elder brain, initially encountered as "No Name." Later identified as Sitherak the Alhoun — possibly one of the Moa, and possibly a lich, having found immortality through the arcane arts. Associated with the Alhoun, a group of illithid growing giant worms in the Underdark.
