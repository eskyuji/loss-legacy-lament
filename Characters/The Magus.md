# The Magus

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World

---

## Overview

Ancient. A Sphinx who sacrificed herself to save The Crash and was resurrected by the Rot against her will, living in constant agonizing pain. Her fungus-covered, wound-seeping form is her prison. Her eyes are scratched out — not physically, but as if someone scratched them out on the paper she was drawn on. Has lived 350 years, watched seven emperors rise and fall, and wants nothing more than to die permanently. Studies Oathstones and Rot magic in search of a way to finally end her suffering.

---

## Campaign 3 Session Detail

Appears first in a humanoid disguise — a golden-haired woman with catlike eyes, sharp nails, and a tail-like belt — before revealing her true, rotted Sphinx form in her Sunning Room. Has spent her centuries researching Oathstones: ancient artifacts/people, created by a one-armed crafter, that bind oaths and may be what's resisted the Nothingness. Theorizes that 11 surviving regions share only 10 Oathstones, meaning two regions (likely The Crash/Azure, and possibly Lost Hope/Rath) split one between them. Believes Varaneth holds the Oathstone of Death (tied to its gate to the afterlife) and that Mythrir itself may be one of several parallel "blueprint" versions of the world.

Asks the party to retrieve a bloodstone from the Scarlet Peaks so she might undo the Rot's hold and finally be allowed to die a less agonizing death. Seraph assists her research by casting Legend Lore.
