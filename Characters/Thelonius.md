# Thelonius

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn, CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

Ancient beyond reckoning, cursed into the form of a sphynx cat by The Watcher for forgetting his duty — a paradox, since his curse is Remembrance. In his true form he is a tall, bald human with a lute and a voice like Frank Sinatra. The Oathstone of Remembrance. Has seen more of Mythrir's history than almost anyone alive and carries it all with a performer's grace and a philosopher's weariness. Meeka is the literal personification of his forgotten duty.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

The Oathstone of Remembrance reveal pays off fully here: now ruling over a dead, abandoned Veranath as a cat hive-mind, Thelonius leads the party through four labors as part of his "Oathstone of Remembrance" arc before they cross the Gates of the Afterlife.

*⚠️ Possible naming overlap: the Campaign 4 raw notes spell this ruined cat-city "Veranath," while the existing [Locations/Varaneth.md](../archive/Locations/Varaneth) entry (capital of the Eternal Empire, home of the Cat's Pajamas, from Campaign 2) is spelled "Varaneth." Given Thelonius's strong ties to both — he holds court at the Cat's Pajamas in the Campaign 2 city and rules the cat hive-mind in the Campaign 4 city — these may be the same city fallen to ruin 2,000 years later, or two distinctly named places. Flagging rather than merging the Locations entries.*

## Campaign 2 Session Detail

Holds court at the Cat's Pajamas in Varaneth, where he's friendly with Laylah and the late barkeep Barry. Runs portal magic and is the one who knights Taeus as his champion after Hagorn the Red's defeat. His song breaks a False Hydra's psychic sway over the party.

---

## Campaign 3 Session Detail

Revealed to be the only known master of all sixteen Metal Magics (Alloymancy), including three newly-discovered metals — Myconian, Serothian, and Tychonian. Covers himself in piercings and rings since he needs metal close to use his powers ("It's not like I can have pockets as a cat."). Takes Tycho's mother, Bailey, on as his apprentice in emotional Metal Magic — she's notably skilled at keeping Maginus in check.

Turns a Scarlet Peaks aggressor who blamed him for the world's end into a sentient dog and forces him into labor. During the blood magic riot in Shaidar's Rest, is gravely wounded and loses an arm — Benji's restorative magic saves his life but erases the arm so completely that ordinary restoration magic can't bring it back; he's fitted with a new pewter prosthetic afterward, paid for by Myka.

*⚠️ Metal-naming note: this section names the three newly-discovered metals "Myconian, Serothian, and Tychonian." Campaign 5 (Session 1) confirms a set of three Vulcan-forged metals under the names "Tychonium, Seraphium, and Myconium." Confirmed by the table: these are the same three, each named for and tied to a specific player character's influence — Tychonium to Tycho, Seraphium to Seraph, and Myconium to Netiri (via her myconid/fungal domain rather than a direct name-match). "Serothian" was evidently an early or informal name for what settled into "Seraphium" — resolved rather than merged silently, per the naming pattern already seen elsewhere in this file (Death's Scythe → Death's Walking Stick).*
