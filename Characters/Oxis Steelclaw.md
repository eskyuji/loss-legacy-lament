## Overview

A seven-foot-ten Dragonborn fighter from one of the three great Dragonborn houses. Built purely for function over fashion. Carries an artifact sword and a battlerifle. Lost his entire clan in the darkened city of The Crash and was found walking out of the darkness alone. Joins the party with a simple, total commitment — until his last breath.
