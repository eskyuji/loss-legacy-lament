# Enki Steelbane

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

A six-foot Tortle druid born in the Ebonywoods. His parents were killed by the dragonborn Hagorn the Red, driving him to join the military (The Fist) and seek strength. Patient, principled, and connected to the natural world. Founded an orphanage (Little Hope, in Varaneth, with Myka). Bonded with a Todea named Atraxa. Eventually masters Wind Walk and becomes one of the most capable members of the Frost Shepherds.

---

## Campaign 2 Session Detail

**Magic items / boons:** Carries a Bag of Holding. Trades 30 maximum hit points at Vincent's Vitals and Vittles (a Yuan-Ti shop in Varaneth that doesn't deal in gold, only vitality) to have a party member's staff enchanted. After the Obelex fight, strikes a deal that lets him stay wildshaped indefinitely.

**Key moments:** Finally confronts and defeats Hagorn the Red in single combat while wildshaped as an Earth Elemental, finishing him with a diamond-encrusted hand. Recovers the heart of an Onyx dragon, later passed to Taeus for the land-restoration ritual. Speaks Draconic and Aquan.
