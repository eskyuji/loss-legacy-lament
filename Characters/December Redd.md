# December Redd

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

A shifter whose eyes glow. A martial artist obsessed with pushing his body, mind, and spirit to their absolute limit — less interested in heroism than in finding opponents worthy of him. His driving motivation is his missing daughter, Desiree. Has ten bird tattoos that appeared on his body during training, each corresponding to one of the ancient guilds of Mythrir. Later discovers his daughter is a Truthspeaker.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Leaves Don Carlo Iro a training regime before departing Shaidar's Rest to search for Desiree. The search leads through Lost Hope, the Tyn Street altar, and eventually to a temple in the Maw, where Desiree is found alive — imprisoned and used as a vessel by a version of Feit. Stays anchored to home throughout via calls to his wife, Elise.
