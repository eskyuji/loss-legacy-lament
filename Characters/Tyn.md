# Tyn

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

The Oathstone of Tenacity. An ancient, medieval-looking warrior whose metallic skin is the only thing that distinguishes him from a cosplayer in outdated armor. He was present when Lost Hope was little more than a fifty-foot patch of dirt. His sword Reno — made of pure Tychonium — is worn down to almost nothing from centuries of use. He gives it freely to Bas Tetran before the city goes dark around him.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Met at the altar on Tyn Street in Lost Hope (the street is named for him). Gives Reno to Bas Tetran XXIV without hesitation, just before the city is consumed by darkness around him — see [Reno](../Items/Reno.md).
