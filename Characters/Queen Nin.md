# Queen Nin

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World

---

## Overview

The ruler of The Crash, who views the Frost Shepherds as interlopers. Prickly and territorial but not without dignity — she becomes notably more civil once the party proves their worth. Politically sharp, bound tightly to the traditions of her people.

---

## Campaign 3 Session Detail

Rules from the palace at the heart of The Crash, presiding over a rigid class hierarchy (Rhox at the top, vulture-folk and ratfolk in service roles, catfolk pushed to the slums). Initially hostile, calling The Magus "the usurper" and "the damnable sphynx," and dismisses the party until Myka forces her to kneel with a command-like ability tied to her crown. Considerably warmer the next morning, apologizing for her rudeness and inviting the party to breakfast — floats the idea of an alliance between The Crash and Shaidar's Rest. The party ultimately decides to tell her the full truth about the Magus, Goroshi, and the lich, and to let her choose diplomacy or war on her own.
