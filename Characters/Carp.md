# Carp

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire, CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World

*⚠️ Status: Carp is killed during the blood magic riot in Shaidar's Rest in Campaign 3 (see below) — flagging this status change prominently since his Campaign 2 entry describes him as an active, ongoing party member.*

---

## Overview

**CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire:**

A poison-dart-frog-colored Bullywug with red cheeks, originally hired as a butler by Abacus. Became a fierce fighter and loyal companion, carrying twin revolvers. Found Patricia in the swamps after the apocalypse and started a family — several eggs on the way. Fought alongside Drifter to almost single-handedly defend the Frost Shepherds' keep during a massive Beyond assault (mutated riders mounted on anteater-headed beasts). Received a new gun and a Yeti-shaped cookie cutter as Festival of Lights gifts.

---

## Campaign 3 Session Detail

Found dead, twin revolvers on the ground around him, during the blood magic riot that overruns Shaidar's Rest — one of several major losses in that battle alongside Star (Tycho's squire) and the near-loss of Thelonius. His Greatshell friend Drifter recovers his body. The party later visits Patricia — referred to afterward as his widow — to give their condolences.

*Patricia's fate and the status of their eggs/children are not specified in the notes and remain unconfirmed.*
