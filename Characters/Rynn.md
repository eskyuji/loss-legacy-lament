# Rynn

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

A centaur with a long white beard who has watched over Myka's Keep for three hundred years. Plucked out his own eyes so he wouldn't have to see what lurked inside it — he now sees through the vines on his horn. The human half of his body has no flesh.

---

## Campaign 2 Session Detail

Zeth Nogoth, freed after centuries of imprisonment, feels too ashamed of his absence to face Rynn directly — suggesting a prior bond or duty between them tied to the Keep's defense.
