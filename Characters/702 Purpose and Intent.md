## Overview

A Steel Wrought (Warforged) commander. Mahogany and granite body with red sparkling energy and white diamonds glowing inside. The de facto authority figure who draws the four protagonists together at the start of the campaign. Breaks the Warforged naming convention by having two nouns instead of adjective/noun — suggesting he is something more than standard. Later corrupted and used as a mouthpiece by Feit.
