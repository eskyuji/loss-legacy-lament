# Kwadwo

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World

---

## Overview

**CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World:**

A Rhox infected with the Rot who breaks his people's sacred code by leaving The Crash to seek help — a journey only possible because The Magus gave him a means to travel that far. Snaps off his own horn for the party to deliver to his daughter, Kwa-mina (the Magus passes it along quietly so Queen Nin can't track her). Asks Tycho to make sure he's sent into the Nothingness afterward so he doesn't spread the Rot further. A figure of quiet, devastating sacrifice.
