# Abacus Black

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

A Kalashtar who lives a double life in the Aether while sleeping. Worships the celestial dragon Celestion — nicknamed "the Angler" for his fishing-staff motif. Carries a silver-runed glaive/staff that glows with constellations at night. Has a black spot on his neck from touching a dead god. Cursed with the Curse of Action by the god Urath, picked up after praying at Urath's altar.

---

## Campaign 2 Session Detail

**Magic items:** A Ring of Spell Storing, purchased from a jeweler in Varaneth (the same jeweler — Wesley Valentine — who makes Myka's jewelry).

**Key moments:** Uses Speak with Dead to question fallen enemies. Draws "The Void" from a mysterious deck of cards during a divination scene, releasing a trapped soul. Helps put down a group of psychic Skulks/mages with Sickening Radiance.
