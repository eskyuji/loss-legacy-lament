# Benji

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire, CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World

---

## Overview

Founder of Shaidar's Rest. In death he became the Oathstone of Eternity — his dying promise so powerful it transformed his spirit into a living oath. His body was used by Logan to create the first blood magic, one of the greatest desecrations in Mythrir's history. When restored, he is quiet, warm, and devastatingly competent. His letters contain magic powerful enough to destroy a god's arm.

---

## Campaign 2 Session Detail

One of his letters, carried by Myka, is wished against Logan ("I wish you would disintegrate into nothing") and destroys him outright. Zeth Nogoth was trained as the apprentice of Benji's own apprentice (Logan), one further step removed in the lineage of shadow/blood magic study. Possibly connected to the fifth, still-missing cosmic key (the other four belong to Un'Malo, Yorick, Nala, and Juri).

---

## Campaign 3 Session Detail

Found as the Oathstone of Eternity — body and spirit separated — three floors beneath Shaidar's Rest in the mausoleum, recovered by Sunny and Moony (a pair of cursed relic hunters) for 40,000 gold. His body had been possessed/reanimated by Logan's lingering spirit; once Logan is identified, Benji casts Time Stop to give the party extra turns to finish him off, then helps with the fight against the blood magic riot above. Performs an irreversible restorative spell on the dying Thelonius — strong enough to save his life, but not his arm, which the spell erases so completely that even Greater Restoration-tier magic can't bring it back. Departs afterward to deal with the remaining blood magic cells.

Confirms that Logan's true target wasn't Shaidar's Rest itself but Benji — an attempt to destroy an Oathstone outright — and recommends aluminum as a way to counter the lich Nebbuzar's metal magic.
