# Yelena Moller

*(Yest)*

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 3 — Remnants of a Broken World

---

## Overview

Six-foot-two, 186 pounds. A butch blue Tiefling bard, blue-tinted from being raised by a Frost Giant after her human parents died. Covered in piercings. Got her lute from a traveling salesman and became a bard because she wanted to make people smile. Becomes the inheritor of Goroshi's Oath of Remembrance. Warm, grounded, and quietly formidable.

---

## Campaign 3 Session Detail

Joins the party in The Crash after Goroshi summons her — a "lost soul" with nowhere else to be. Travels with the group through the desert and into Shaidar's Rest. When Goroshi is revealed to be Nebbuzar's phylactery, she's the one who steps up to take his Oath of Remembrance so his protection of the land doesn't simply vanish with him. Fights in the battle against Logan in the catacombs and is present for the devastating losses (Carp, Star, Thelonius's near-death) during the blood magic riot.
