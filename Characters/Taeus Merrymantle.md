# Taeus Merrymantle

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

A Leonin soldier (Monk — fights barehanded with Ki points) promoted to Second Lieutenant early in the campaign, eventually rising to Admiral. Practical, grounded, and dependable. Has tremorsense from his magical stone boots. Deeply tied to the earth and its restoration. Carries out the year-long ritual to restore Mythrir's land using the heart of an Onyx dragon.

---

## Campaign 2 Session Detail

**Magic Items:**
- **The Earth Plate** — small stone necklace; bonus action to become a living suit of stone armor (+2 AC, expertise on Dex/Str saves, attackers have disadvantage on ranged attacks against him). Grants tremorsense for 10 minutes, twice per long rest.
- **Queen's Boots** — snakeskin boots with stone-slab soles, made by a mysterious Yuan-Ti leatherworker who vanished the moment the work was finished. Lets him water-walk via conjured earthen pillars, once per day.
- **Chitin Swordbreaker** — looted from the animated statue of Un'Malo in the Hall of the Owls.

**Companion:** Hercules Jr., a bonded Chiterer.

**Family:** Sister Nazema; mentor Master Korsol.

**Key moments:** Knighted as Thelonius's champion after Hagorn the Red is defeated — a pawprint burns into his armor and he gains the Find Familiar spell. Plants Myconid spores by the urn of Nagi in the Hall of the Owls as a gesture of respect; a cool breeze from the urn is taken as a good omen. Recovers the heart of an Onyx dragon after the fight against the Dark Bringer, which he later uses to perform the ritual restoring Mythrir's land.
