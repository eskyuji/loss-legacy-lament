# The Emperor (Za'a-ni)

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

An arrogant, scrawny, armless Leonin armorer artificer, only eighteen years old. Small enough to be mistaken for a regular Tabaxi. Wears a black trench coat and red visor. Think Dr. Disrespect crossed with a young Tony Stark. Runs a scrapyard, commands a 16-year-old goblin named Russo, and compensates for his physical limitations with technological brilliance and insufferable confidence. Descended from a long imperial line — the moon Yeziah is named after his bloodline.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Warns Russo to evacuate the Scrapyard when Shaidar's Rest goes dark, and Russo heeds it. Credits Master Tinkerer Morrin's tools as the foundation of his own craft. Granted the downtime feature "Will of the Emperor." Appears in Bas Tetran's dream sequence during Session 0.5 alongside the old Emperor of Mythrir, hinting at a thematic link between his title and the "Halls of Yeziah" vision.
