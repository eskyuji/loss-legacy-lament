## Overview

The God with a capital G. Half-orc with sickly green skin, a wooden prosthetic arm, and a massive, battered sword. Created seventy-three worlds. Carries a jar of True Ice containing a fragment of The Beyond. Quiet, tired, and immeasurably old. The Beyond is his great enemy and Feit — the shed order of Yogg-Sothoth — is his world's specific curse. Promises that with Feit gone, he will let Mythrir finally reach the stars.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Delivers the campaign's central cosmological lore-dump: he is the World Crafter, Un'Malo (the Fourth Aspect, killed in Campaign 2) was one of his Aspects, and Feit is "the Dark Door" — order shed from Yogg-Sothoth and bound specifically to Mythrir as its curse. Reveals that Orthel was his own shed madness, since usurped by Feit, and that True Ice (the substance in his jar) is one of the only known weapons effective against Feit. Promises the stars to Mythrir once Feit is gone.
