# Tyra

*(The Sword of Typhlo)*

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire, CAMPAIGN 1 — Guilds in the Dark

---

## Overview

A tall blonde woman encountered deep in the Hall of the Owls. Mid-forties, tarnished armor, golden hair that is literally on fire along with her sword and her tabard. Second Sword of the dead god Typhlo. Has spent eternity in the Owls' Tower fighting the tentacle creatures that killed her friends, as her penance.

*Note: a statue inscription in the Hall of the Owls credits her title to "the second sword of Urath" rather than Typhlo. Campaign 1 material confirms Typhlo and Urath are two distinct Twin Creator Gods of the Underdark — Avatars of Fire and Water respectively — whose mutual battle created the Underdark; the player's raw notes describe Urath as killed by Typhlo, who then buried himself in a volcano. Given that, the statue's "Urath" inscription looks like a genuine discrepancy rather than alternate naming for the same figure — still flagging rather than picking one, since which title is "correct" hasn't been confirmed in session.*

## Campaign 1 Session Detail

Not encountered directly, but the underdark's lore of Typhlo and Urath — the "Twin Gods" she is sword to — is established here in full: Urath, god of water, was killed by Typhlo; Typhlo then dragged his sword to a volcano near the town named for him and buried himself inside it, and the battle between the two gods is what created the Underdark itself.
