# Loanar

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn

---

## Overview

An ancient goddess of silver roses. Appears in a white void as a woman with silver, vine-like braided hair sitting inside a giant silver rose. Was imprisoned for a time before being freed by adventurers. Watches over Mythrir from between realms. Calm, vast, and only occasionally moved to anger — usually when someone mishandles her roses.

---

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Central to the Tycho/Myka death-and-deification interlude in her realm: Tycho is confirmed as the God of Death and Myka is remade as Vengeance Incarnate under Loanar's domain. Also tied to the lore of the Moa pendants distributed by Meeka at her bazaar — four silver-rose-marked tokens whose full purpose is still being established in session.
