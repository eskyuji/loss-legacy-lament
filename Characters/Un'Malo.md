# Un'Malo

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

The Fourth Aspect of the World Crafter — the Aspect of Vengeance. A black dragonborn with a twenty-foot wingspan. Exiled and unrecognized by the Empire's official religion. Deeply powerful, frequently furious, and ultimately mortal. Killed by a child carrying a shadow-dripping blade at the worst possible moment. His death marks the end of the Age of Gods.

---

## Campaign 2 Session Detail

An animated statue of him stands in the Hall of the Owls; the party loots it after his death — Taeus takes a Chitin Swordbreaker, Abacus takes a piece of chitin armor, and Enki keeps a shield. Holds one of the five cosmic keys (the others are tied to Yorick, Nala, Juri, and a fifth missing key later connected to Benji).
