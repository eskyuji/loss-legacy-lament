# Myka

*(The Silver Rose / Vengeance Incarnate)*

**Type:** Major Character  
**Campaign Appearances:** CAMPAIGN 4 — Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn, CAMPAIGN 2 — The Eternal Empire

---

## Overview

The First Mistress of Shaidar's Rest, resurrected after 2,000 years to serve as the vengeance of the gods. A blue-tinted Tiefling with silver, vine-like braided hair and wings of shadow. In life she built an empire; in death she made a deal with Loanar to return when the new Frost Shepherds need her most. Fierce, regal, and deeply loyal to Shaidar's Rest. Wields a blade of shadow and carries the quiet grief of someone who has outlived everything she ever loved.

*Note: the original compendium blurb placed her only under the (mislabeled) "Campaign 1" tag — actually Campaign 4's "Past and Future, Dusk and Dawn." She is also a major active party member throughout the Campaign 2 — The Eternal Empire notes (see below), so her appearances cover both. There is no separate Campaign 1 material on file; the user has confirmed Campaign 1 notes have not yet been uploaded.*

## Campaign 4 Session Detail

Dies alongside Tycho and is remade in Loanar's realm as Vengeance Incarnate — the deal she struck in death finally called due, roughly 2,000 years after the events of Campaigns 2–3. Touches Aleer's divine blood. Present for the Seraph rematch and redemption, the journey to the Tower of the Third Moon aboard Aleer's airship the Heracles, and the final confrontation with Geno Atul at Empyr.

*⚠️ Note: this entry already spans Campaigns 2–3; Campaign 4 takes place roughly 2,000 years after that era, so this file now covers a very long arc for one character across radically different periods of her existence (mortal ruler → resurrected vengeance-bringer → ascended god). Flagging for awareness rather than splitting the file, since it's clearly the same continuous identity.*

## Campaign 2 Session Detail

A bow-wielding fighter with some divine casting (Cure Wounds; Polymorphs into a Giant Ape to land a killing blow against a False Hydra). Originally from the ruins of Nuxvar.

**Magic items / boons:** Trades her left eye in a Yuan-Ti deal — now sees creatures from the Beyond that others can't, at the cost of a permanently "frosted" eye. Receives a snakeskin quiver and a cloak that shifts between several magical forms day to day from the same deal. Gains a special arrow upgrade after the fight with Obelex, and a Hund (warbeast) she names Hades. Wears armor commissioned from Goods & Glorious Gains.

**Key moments:** Carries letters from Benji containing magic powerful enough to destroy a god's arm; uses one to wish Logan out of existence ("I wish you would disintegrate into nothing"), ending his vampiric shadow-lord reign over Myka's Keep.

## Campaign 3 Session Detail

Formally accepts her role ruling Shaidar's Rest, now a flourishing, diverse city — the third most powerful city-state in Mythrir. Sets four thrones in the throne room, deliberately left empty. Rynn grows her a suit of Ironwood Armor over the downtime (AC 10 + Dex + Wis, no stealth disadvantage).

In The Crash, uses an ability tied to her crown (functioning like a Command spell) to force Queen Nin to kneel during a tense first meeting. Later recovers Benji's urn/medallion from the Mausoleum beneath Shaidar's Rest, reuniting with him as the Oathstone of Eternity. Encounters Meeka twice in the desert via a silver rosebud, which tells her "The Lost One seeks for you to join her" — declines further contact with Meeka after Meeka threatens to take her quiver, bow, name, and friends. Prays to Loanar (the silver rose goddess) to ensure Thelonius's recovery after he's nearly killed, offering "anything in return" — a vow Benji immediately warns her never to make to a god.

When the blood magic riot breaks out in Shaidar's Rest, chooses to stay and fight for the city over immediately pursuing the Oathstone in the catacombs.
